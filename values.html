Core values

Guided by dignity, equality, and community power

These values ground every PRODEVIOW program and partnership, keeping us accountable to the women and girls who lead this movement.

Respect for Human Dignity

Every person deserves to be valued and treated with respect.

Integrity & Accountability

Transparency and responsibility guide our actions.

Gender Equality

We advocate for equal rights, opportunities, and representation.

Compassion & Empathy

We lead with understanding, humanity, and survivor-centered care.

Community-Driven Solutions

Local ownership and collective impact power sustainable change.

Challenges we face

We act because violence, poverty, and inequality persist

Violence

GBV remains widespread in displacement settings. We provide safe reporting pathways, dialogues, and survivor accompaniment.

Poverty

Limited work permits make livelihoods difficult. We equip women with portable skills, savings groups, and entrepreneurship support.

Inequality

Women and girls are underrepresented in leadership. We cultivate advocacy, civic participation, and gender equality education.

Lack of economic opportunity

Safe, inclusive learning spaces are scarce. Play & Grow nurtures children with creativity, empathy, and opportunity.
